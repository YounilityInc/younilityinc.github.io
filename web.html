Dispatching, scheduling, and time tracking made simple

One simple interface with live reports

Dispatching and scheduling

Dispatching work to employees and scheduling their activity has never been easier with intuitive mapping and calendar view as well as “drag and drop” ordering.

Reports and file storage New

Keeping track of who does what and when can be a real hassle. Younility reports track every activity from the moment a task starts until it ends. We also store that data forever.

User location history

Ever needed to know where someone has been during the day or at anytime in the past? We track the mobile device’s location history and direction of movement.

Time tracking New

Knowing how much time has been spent on a task is vital for understanding labor hours, task completion, and billing. Simply start, stop, or pause a task and we’ll track the time for you.

Live employee tracking

As soon as an employee logs into the mobile app Younility will track their location. You can dispatch more efficiently knowing who is closest to a task and when they will arrive.

Import / export data New

If you already have a database of locations or assets that are in another system, simply import them into Younility. Exporting allows you to send work activity to your CRM or accounting system.
