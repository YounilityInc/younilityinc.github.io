Privacy Policy

Privacy – Younility Inc.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Field A.I.R. (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Younility Inc. The Application is designed to allow updating of the Ericsson Adaptive Inventory (EAI) system in real time. Field A.I.R. focuses on the field by permitting real time data collection using mobile devices and the Field A.I.R. Application. Tasks can be sent directly from EAI to field technicians, allowing them to audit information and return it to EAI. Built in maps and turn-by-turn directions allow field technicians to navigate to their work sites. Attachments allow field users or admin users to attach photos and files to send between dispatch and the field. A wide range of information can be sent to the field for verification or information purposes.

Field A.I.R. is comprised of three main components: the Younility Application Program Interface (API), the Field A.I.R. Bridge and the Field A.I.R. mobile Application. Together they form the full solution, able to translate data collection requests and generate task requests from EAI to the field technician.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

During use of Field A.I.R., user-generated content such as photos and files can be transmitted via Field A.I.R. to Ericsson Adaptive Inventory (EAI). Photos are stored locally, encrypted locally and encrypted remotely. Younility does not intentionally share your files with any third parties.

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download and register the Application.

When you register with us and use the Application, you generally provide (a) your name, email address, password and other registration information; (b) information you provide us when you contact us for help; and; (c) information you enter into our system when using the Application, such as contact information and project management information.

We may also use the information you provided us to contact your from time to time to provide you with important information, required notices and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, your location and information about the way you use the Application.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

When you visit the mobile application, we may use GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current location in order to determine the city you are located within and display a location map to provide directions and distances to work sites. We will not share your current location with other users or partners.

Use of the Google Geolocation API provides access to maps and turn-by-turn directions, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for further information. Location services can be disabled by the user, but may be required by the employer for the use of the Application.

If you do not want us to use your location for the purposes set forth above, you should turn off the location services for the mobile application located in your account settings or in your mobile phone settings and/or within the mobile application.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Yes. We will share your information with third parties only in the ways that are described in this privacy statement.
We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process;
  • when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request;
  • with our trusted services providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent use of the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this privacy statement.
  • if Younility Inc. is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, you will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our Web site of any change in ownership or uses of this information, as well as any choices you may have regarding this information.

What are my opt-out rights?

Users can opt not to enable camera or calling access; but this will affect the user’s ability to attach photographs to tasks when required, or call their dispatcher directly from the Application when applicable.

Location Services can be disabled under settings, which will prevent use of the Google Geolocation API.

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network. You can also request to opt-out via email, at support@younility.com.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. We will retain Automatically Collected information for up to 24 months and thereafter may store it in aggregate. If you’d like us to delete User Provided Data that you have provided via the Application, please contact us at support@younility.com and we will respond in a reasonable time. Please note that some or all of the User Provided Data may be required in order for the Application to function properly.
