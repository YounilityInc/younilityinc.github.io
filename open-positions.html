About Younility

Younility is a small and rapidly-growing software startup in the web and mobile space. Our Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product, Younility, is leading the charge in making mobile workers more effective and productive. Our customers love the time we save them every day and our partners know we’re onto something big.

We are experiencing a surge in new signups and we require creative, intelligent, and focused developers to tackle a significant workload of new features. Candidates must be local although we will consider telecommuting one day a week for the right candidate. No agencies, 3rd parties or recruiters.

We may be a small company now, but we can offer you a great company culture and work environment (which is very important to us), a competitive salary, profit sharing, and meaningful equity in the company. We’re located at 20 Camelot in Nepean, next to a cartoon factory and some of the best greasy breakfast spots in the city.

Senior Software Developer

Position: Senior Software Developer
Location: Ottawa, ON

Description

Our web team needs developers to help build our Ruby on Rails SaaS app using test-driven development and continuous integration. This means programming in Ruby, CoffeeScript, HTML5, and CSS. You will work closely with the mobile team to keep in sync with changes to the API and development of features which cross the divide.

Requirements
  • Minimum 3 years professional experience writing web applications
  • Preference will be given to those with RoR and scripting experience
Skills and Assets
  • Experience programming web services and RESTful web APIs
  • Experience configuring and optimizing back-end concerns like databases, memcache, mailers, file storage, and scheduled tasks
  • Integrating 3rd-party HTTP APIs and/or SDKs (e.g., Stripe, S3)
  • Test frameworks (e.g., minitest, rspec)
  • Continuous integration servers (e.g., Jenkins, Circle, Travis)
  • App deployment (e.g., to heroku, aws, etc)
  • Bootstrap, CSS, and responsive design would be an asset

To apply please send your cover letter and resume to careers@younility.com
